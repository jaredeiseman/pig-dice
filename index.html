  • Once you're satisfied with your turns run score, you can hold to add your run score to your total score.
  • The first player to --- points wins!
How to use our app:
  • To roll your dice, give the spinning piggy a nice welcoming click!
  • Ready to hold? Tell that badboy spinning hold button to stop by clicking 'em!
  • The game will tell you when someone has won!
